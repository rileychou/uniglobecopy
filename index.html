notite

a family away from home

take a break from what's going on

here at notite, we realize that home is not necessarily a safe place for everyone. with covid-19, it forces many people to stay under the same roofs with their abusers. notite wants to provide a safe home for youth, no matter where you are in the world.

everything you need to know, all in one place

not sure how to cope with your struggles? want to explore or donate to charities? notite is your ultimate info station! we have helpful info, links, and resources that might help you!

games are the way

notite wants to make a positive impact on our users in more ways than one. that's why we have fun mini games, designed by our expert developers, to give you something to focus on. we have a selection of games for you to choose from so you can work on beating your high score, or just simply relax.

you are not alone

the upside of joining the notite community is that you can meet people who understand what you're going through! this community can offer advice, share helpful tips, or just simply chat and have fun. we truly are a family.
