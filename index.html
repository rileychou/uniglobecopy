take a break from what's going on

here at remarque, we realize that home is not necessarily a safe place for everyone. with covid-19, it forces many people to stay under the same roofs with their abusers. remarque wants to provide a safe home for youth, no matter where you are in the world.

not sure how to cope with your struggles? want to explore or donate to charities? remarque is your ultimate info station! we have helpful info, links, and resources that might help you!

the upside of joining the remarque community is that you can meet people who understand what you're going through! this community can offer advice, share helpful tips, or just simply chat and have fun. we truly are a family.
