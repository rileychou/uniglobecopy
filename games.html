games

Pong is a game where you hit a ball back and forth. If the ball lands beyond the opponent's reach, you get a point! First to 5 points wins, and a new round starts. The game will get faster as time passes.

Rock, paper, scissors is a well known game. Paper covers rock, rock crushes scissors, and scissors cuts paper. If you win the round, you get a point!
